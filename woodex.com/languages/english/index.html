THE ADVANTAGES OF OUR LUMBER

The main advantage of any wood is naturalness and toxic safety. Any synthetic analogues cannot exceed these properties. However, lumber from spruce has its undeniable advantages:

  -  Lightness, low density and low thermal conductivity;
  -  Small diameter knots create an interesting pattern;
  -  The unusual color of the wood looks original in the interior. Spruce can give a light pink or light gold shade;
  -  The homogeneous texture gives the timber incomparable evenness and smoothness.
